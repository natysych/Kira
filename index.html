Favourite recipes:

Apple maffines

Ingredients:

  • 1 apple
  • 200 gr flour
  • 100 gr butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 6 spoon of sugar
  • 15 gr baking powder
  • vanilla, cinnamon, nuts

Add eggs and sugar to softened butter, mix thoroughly. In a separate bowl, mix flour, vanilla, baking powder and ground cinnamon. Combine the ingredients and carefully mix the apple chopped into small cubes into the dough. Add nuts. Insert corrugated paper into silicone molds. Pour the dough in molds and bake for 30 minutes at 180 °C. About half an hour - and your home will be filled with the spicy aroma of cinnamon and apples.
Fill the molds with dough only ¾ - the muffins will rise during baking.

Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 115 gr butter (room temperature)
  • 190 gr sugar
  • 100 gr butter
  • 1 egg
  • 105 gr flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 130 gr rolled oats
  • salt, vanilla, cinnamon, raisins

    • Preheat your oven to 190°C. Then line to large cookie sheets with parchment paper and set aside. Combine the dry ingredients, apart from the sugars. Set aside. In a large bowl cream together the butter and sugar. Once light and fluffy add the egg and the vanilla and whip until combined. Add the dry ingredients into the wet and mix, do not over mix, fold in the oats and the raisins.
    Bake for about 9-10 minutes or until the cookies are golden brown and slightly risen
